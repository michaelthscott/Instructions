Late at night in bed\
lying on your right side curled up\
comfortable\
limbs consciously positioned\
right foot hooked around your left\
right hand on the pillow in front but not touching your face\
first finger and thumb together to form an O\
other three fingers curled into the palm\
left hand in loosely clenched fist\
placed between knees and right elbow\
eyes closed looking at the vague dark and colour\
rub the tips of the finger and thumb to and fro\
listen to the sound\
through the pillow\
and concentrate on seeing and believing\
your imagination of the action
