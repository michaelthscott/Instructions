Find\
moderately busy pavement\
edged by wall

loiter against it\
facing down the street

select an attractive person\
from those approaching

fix intently\
the chosen one\
in your eyes\
an instant

then

moving neither head nor eyes\
attempt to follow\
a feeling of their presence\
passing\
out\
of vision

*
