Morning\
lying in bed\
look towards the ceiling

place the first three fingers\
of the left hand\
against the side edge of the left eye socket\
thumb on the corner of the jaw

look at your palm\
and\
at the same time lightly apply pressure\
with the little finger of the left hand\
to the top right hand corner of your eyelid

watch the spot appearing in the lower left\
