series: Instructions

While awaiting slumber\
lie on your back\
head and shoulders well propped upon the pillow\
legs crossed\
one ankle over the other\
hands folded on your stomach\
look unto the back of your eyelids\
as if the sight were an entertainment

try hard and continuously\
to concentrate upon the black\
that lies behind the phantoms of colour and light\
watching for a single point of light\
appearing alone\
surrounded by black

focus\
trying to prevent it from fading\
when faded wait for it to return
